TV Energy Consumption

Energy Consumption for different TV screen
types and sizes

Most TVs on sale in Australia are LED and they vary considerably in energy consumption.

TVs with the same star rating can have very different energy consumption. It all depends on screen size and technology type.

Only TVs with energy consumption less than 1,800 kWh/year have been included.
